How A Dragon Hoards

Plot? There is one, sort of.

How A Dragon Hoards is about a magical prince named Otto, who can talk to animals and uses them as an intelligence network in exchange for food. On the day of his mother and father's coronation, Prince Otto is kidnapped by a dragon that swoops down to take him to the Dragonlands and hold him hostage against the kingdom of Luteria. Not one to take this lying down, Otto thus embarks on an adventure in which he must save his kingdom with the help of his friends, both old and new. It's not really that serious, and I promise the title makes a lot more sense later on.
